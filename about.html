About Sattvavajaya Chikitsa

Sattvavajaya Chikitsa is a unique approach to mind-body wellness rooted in the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda. Based in Pune, our center is dedicated to promoting holistic health, emotional balance, and spiritual well-being through traditional and integrative therapies.

At our meditation center, we embrace the wisdom of Sattvavajaya Chikitsa—an ancient Ayurvedic Physio - neurological therapy that gently guides you back to balance. Rooted in the belief that true health begins with the mind, this practice helps restore inner harmony by nurturing clarity (Sattva) and calming the restlessness of excess activity (Rajas) or heaviness (Tamas).

Our Philosophy

Sattvavajaya Chikitsa, as described in classical Ayurvedic texts, is the science of mind control and emotional management. We believe that true health is achieved by harmonizing the mind, body, and spirit. Our therapies are designed to help individuals overcome stress, anxiety, depression, and other psychosomatic disorders by restoring inner balance and cultivating positive mental states.


  • Personalized Ayurvedic counseling and therapy
  • Integration of yoga, meditation, and lifestyle guidance
  • Focus on emotional resilience and spiritual growth
  • Evidence-based and compassionate care
