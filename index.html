Discover Sattvavajaya Chikitsa

Sattvavajaya is not just a type of treatment—it is a way to live better. We use meditation, yoga, simple mindfulness, talking one-on-one, and daily healthy habits. These steps help you become stronger inside, know yourself better, and live a happier life.

Sattvavajaya Chikitsa is a method to help your mind feel better. It uses meditation, yoga, simple talks, and good habits to help you feel calm, happy, and less worried.

Anyone who wants to feel more peaceful, less stressed, or happier can try Sattvavajaya Chikitsa. It is good for people feeling worried, sad, or just wanting a better life.

In our sessions, we do simple meditation, yoga, breathing, talk with you, and help you with easy daily habits. We choose what is best for you and make it easy to follow.
